# System Overview

OurGlass is a first-of-its-kind Hebrew AI-powered financial platform designed specifically for couples in Israel. It goes beyond traditional budgeting tools by treating joint finances as a shared journey, providing deep insights, real-time tracking, and proactive AI assistance.

## Core Philosophy

Joint finances are often a source of stress. OurGlass aims to transform this into a source of connection by:
- **Transparency**: A single source of truth for all shared assets and expenses.
- **Proactivity**: AI that points out trends before they become problems.
- **Aesthetics**: A premium, Hebrew-first interface that makes financial management feel modern and approachable.

## Key Features

- **Net Worth Engine**: Real-time calculation of net worth across cash, stocks, real estate, and liabilities.
- **AI Agent "Roee"**: A conversational assistant that can record transactions, analyze spending, and answer complex financial questions.
- **Wealth History**: Visual progression of the couple's financial health over time.
- **Smart Insights**: Daily, AI-generated "roasts" or "praises" based on total committed spending (Variable + Fixed).
- **Yield Accrual**: Automatic compounding of interest for savings and money market funds.
- **Market Data**: Live integration for stock prices and USD/ILS exchange rates.

## Target Audience

Designed for Israeli couples who want a modern, mobile-friendly way to manage their joint household economy, specifically optimized for Hebrew speakers and the local financial ecosystem.

## Language Support

> [!NOTE]
> The application UI, user-facing notifications, and AI interactions are **entirely in Hebrew**. All internal documentation is in English for developer accessibility.
